## Gaps

| Category     | Gap                          | Why it should exist (mini/replica value)                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 📱 Portable  | Game & Watch (Single Screen) | Strongest hardware identity in handheld history; perfect for a multi-game compilation device; currently fragmented across releases |
| 📱 Portable  | Game & Watch (Multi Screen)  | Introduced D-pad + dual screen concept; direct ancestor to Nintendo DS; iconic form factor worth recreating                        |
| 💻 Computers | MS-DOS / IBM PC compatibles  | Defines global PC gaming with massive library; would enable a “unified DOS gaming box” experience                                  |
| 💻 Computers | MSX                          | Strong, cohesive ecosystem (especially Konami); clear identity; bridges Japan/Europe computing history                             |
| 💻 Computers | NEC PC-98 (PC-88 adjacent)   | Dominant Japanese PC platform with unique library; fills a completely missing regional perspective                                 |
| 🕹️ Arcade    | Capcom CPS (CPS1/2/3)        | Cohesive hardware ecosystem + massive iconic library (fighters, beat ‘em ups); **best candidate for an arcade mini**               |
| 🕹️ Arcade    | Namco                        | Foundational arcade company with huge library; would represent early arcade era in one package                                     |
| 🕹️ Arcade    | Konami                       | Strong arcade identity (co-op, shooters, licensed games); fills gap in arcade diversity not covered by Sega/SNK/Taito              |
